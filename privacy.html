Introduction
Introduction

Welcome to MLNO! We are committed to protecting your privacy and your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, products, or services. By using our platform, you consent to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information Collection

We collect a variety of information to provide and improve our services. This information includes personal data that you provide when signing up for an account or contacting us such as your names & email addresses, non-personal information like browser type and device information, and information collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, including your device type and IP addresses.

How Information is Collected

Personal information is collected when you voluntarily provide it to us through registration forms, contact forms, or other interactions with our website and / or when we interact in person or via other virtual contact mechanisms like video calls etc. Non-personal information is gathered through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies. Automatic information collection occurs as you browse our website.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect personal information to create and manage user accounts, process orders, and provide customer support, and to send you updates and newsletters. Non-personal information helps us improve our website, personalize content, and understand user preferences. We may also use data for marketing, research, and analytical purposes.

Sharing of Information

Your personal information may be shared with trusted third parties, including payment processors, shipping partners, and email service providers, to facilitate the delivery of our services. Third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for specified purposes. We do not sell or rent your data to advertisers.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content. You can manage or opt-out of cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that certain site features may be impacted if you disable cookies.
